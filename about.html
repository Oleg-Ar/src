About me

I’m a graphic designer who genuinely loves turning ideas into visual stories. For me, design is not just about making things look good—it’s about creating something that feels right, communicates clearly, and connects with people.

I’ve always been drawn to creativity, whether that’s developing brand identities, experimenting with typography and layout, or exploring different styles like collage and mixed media. I enjoy the whole process—from the first spark of an idea to the final polished piece—and I’m always looking for new ways to push my work further.

Over time, I’ve built strong skills in tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and After Effects, which help me bring my ideas to life across both print and digital projects. But just as important to me is the human side of design: listening, understanding, and working closely with others to create something meaningful.

Before moving fully into design, I gained experience in customer service and management, which taught me how to stay organised, communicate clearly, and handle challenges calmly—skills I carry with me into every creative project.

At the heart of it, I simply enjoy creating. I like finding solutions, shaping ideas, and making things that people can connect with and remember.
